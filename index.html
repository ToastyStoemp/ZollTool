What this tool covers

  • Temporary import declaration into Switzerland (Formular 11.74)
  • E-dec re-export declaration for unsold goods
  • Sold goods tracking and customs value calculation

What this tool does NOT cover

  • Export documents from your home country — you are still required to obtain the correct export paperwork from the customs authority of the country you are based in before travelling to Switzerland.
  • Proof of EU/home-country origin — keep your export documents safe. You will need them to prove the goods originated from the EU (or your country) when re-importing your unsold stock after the event.
  • Any goods permanently sold or left behind in Switzerland.

Your privacy

This tool runs entirely in your browser. No data is stored on any server. No data is collected. Everything you enter stays on your device.
